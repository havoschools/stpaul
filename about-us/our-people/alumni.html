Alumni

Greetings from St. Paul Christian Academy and welcome, alumni! I am Amy Richardson, Director of Annual Fund and Alumni Relations, and while I am new to this position and new to St. Paul, I am not new to Nashville or this role. I have lived in Nashville for over twenty years and have over ten years of experience in development and alumni relations with another independent school here in Nashville. My two children have also joined the St. Paul family: Laurel (Class of 2027) and Garrett (Class of 2029). We look forward to continuing to be part of this special community.

This is a very exciting year for the school, and we want to invite you to be a part of it, reconnect with classmates and teachers, and help us celebrate St. Paul’s 50th Anniversary. As we kick off the 50th Anniversary Celebration of the St. Paul experience, Will Norton, Head of School, and I look forward to getting to know you. We invite you to share with us any news you have about yourself—employment updates, special awards or recognitions, or life events such as getting married or becoming a parent. We just want to celebrate you through any milestone, big or small!

Share Your Good News Here

We’re so grateful that your story is a part of our story, and I hope to have the opportunity to connect with you in the future. In the meantime, I wish you the best and am happy to answer any questions about St. Paul!
