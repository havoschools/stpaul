Lower School

Lower School begins in junior kindergarten and continues through second grade. The early elementary years are incredibly formative for children’s academic, social, and spiritual growth. We immerse these young students in a supportive and nurturing learning environment. We seek to cultivate active and creative learners through a developmentally appropriate curriculum, creating habits and values that are relevant and long-lasting.

 

“Nothing brings me greater joy than collaborating with students, parents, and teachers to foster academically excellent, Christ-centered childhood classrooms. I consider it a privilege to partner with families as they make the exciting decision of where to send their child to school. At St. Paul, our entire team encourages curiosity and a lifelong love of learning in our students.”

- Amy Nickels
