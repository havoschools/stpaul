Summer Work

Happy summer! We hope you are enjoying the outdoors, swimming, sleeping in late, being together as a family, and, of course, having a great time at St. Paul Summer Camps! We hope you have a wonderful few months ahead of you and that you can create many memories during this time. We also hope you spend some time keeping your child’s memory fresh with all the important things they learned this past school year. Below, you will find summer worksheets for your child to complete, as well as reading lists for each grade-level. We recommend all of our families to purchase their summer reading books from our friends at Logos Christian Bookstore in Green Hills.

The grades below contain work for the school year your child will be entering in fall 2023. Some grades require that summer work be turned in on the first day of school: August 16, 2023.
